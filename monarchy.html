How to Become a Monarch

Understand the monarchy system and the path to becoming a monarch or a member of the royal court.

  • Monarchy System: Structure and roles within the monarchy.
  • Requirements: Qualifications for royal titles.
  • Duties: Responsibilities of the monarch and court members.

How to Become a Monarchy Member?

Duke

  • You need to have bought 30 chunks of land.
  • You need to have more than xxxk in the bank.
  • A minimum of 20 houses on your land.
  • Need to have some infrastructure on your land (e.g., roads).
  • Need to have some crop fields on your land.
  • Have some military power (e.g., cannons, trebuchets).
  • Need to get approved by the royal family.

Count

  • You need to have bought 20 chunks of land.
  • You need to have more than xxxk in the bank.
  • A minimum of 10 houses on your land.
  • Need to have some infrastructure on your land (e.g., roads).
  • Need to have some crop fields on your land.
  • Have some military power (e.g., cannons, trebuchets).
  • Need to get approved by the royal family.

Vis-Count

  • You need to have bought 15 chunks of land.
  • You need to have more than xxxk in the bank.
  • A minimum of 5 houses on your land.
  • Need to have some infrastructure on your land (e.g., roads).
  • Need to have some crop fields on your land.
  • Have some military power (e.g., cannons, trebuchets).
  • Need to get approved by the royal family.
