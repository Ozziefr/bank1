Business Creation

Start your own business in our country with ease. Learn about the steps, requirements, and incentives available for business owners.

  • Requirements:
    • You need to be a citizen to have a business.
    • You need to have a minimum of $xxk.
    • You need to have a minimum of 4 chunks of land.
    • You need to deposit a minimum of $xxk in the bank.
  • Incentives:
    • Free business creation for deposits over xxxk.
    • Your taxes will be lowered to xx$.
  • After You Created Your Business:
    • Specify what you are going to sell.
    • Report everything you sold and for what price (5% will be taken as a fee).
    • Provide the following details:
      • Business name
      • What you will be selling
      • The money you have in your balance
      • The money you have in the bank
      • How many people will be working in the business
  • Support: Resources and assistance for new businesses.
